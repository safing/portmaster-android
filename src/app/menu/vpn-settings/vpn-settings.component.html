System Settings Guide

To be protected at all times, quickly make sure Portmaster is always on:

1. Tap the next to Portmaster.






2. Enable "Always-on VPN". This makes sure Portmaster is always running, even after a restart.
Optional: For further privacy, enable "Block connections without VPN". This setting prevents any connections from accessing the Internet outside of Portmaster. Even those excluded in "Apps Settings".
