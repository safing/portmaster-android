System Settings

How to turn on always-on VPN and block connections outside of the VPN service:

Tap the next to the Portmaster icon.



By enabling this setting the system will make sure that Portmaster is always running, even after restart.
This setting will prevent apps accessing the internet outside of Portmaster.
This will appear if you have multiple profiles and will enable the Portmaster VPN service for all profiles.
